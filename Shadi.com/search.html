Search Details

Hello! Welcome to shadi®.com Search Options. You can perform unlimited number of searches by selecting the parameters of your choice. Enter gender, age range, religion, country etc. It is quite simple try variety of searches for your ideal match.

To be able to Use Search Options you must be a Registered Member. To register click here

High priority is given to the profiles of the 5 star members followed by other Paid Members lists on all the search displays.

Quick Search: This gives you results in only 4 steps to get the basic display lists of all the members. Instant matches based on your preferences settings.

Advance Search: Detailed search refines your results based on your selection criteria to get the fine ultimate displays for your ideal match. More specific search with your selective preferences set by you.

Search by Country: You can search for your life Partner worldwide. Enter gender, religion and caste, country name and age range e.g.: male, Hindu Brahmin, USA, 25-30. The results will only be from United States .

Search by Profession: Specific profession wise search for those members who are keen to find ideal match from a preferred Profession e.g.: Doctors, Engineers, Architects and others based on your selection.

Search by Profile Id: Every profile at shadi®.com has a Profile Id number unique for that profile. If you know the number just save time and type the profile id to get the result in seconds.

Search by religion and caste: Enter the religion and caste and get the desired displays quickly. No need to browse other profiles which do not meet your requirement for the ideal match.

Enjoy this interesting feature favorite on shadi®.com for our paid members of 4 star and 5 star memberships.
