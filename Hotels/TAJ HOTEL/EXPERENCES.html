EXPERIENCES

CURATED EXPERIENCES BY TAJ

Discover an exquisite collection of experiences—romantic escapes, family delights, bold adventures and signature indulgences. Each is thoughtfully crafted to immerse you in the warmth, charm and elegance that define every stay.
CHAMPAGNE BREAKFAST IN THE BOUGAINVILLEA GARDEN
Enjoy a once-in-a-lifetime breakfast experience at Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jodhpur, surrounded by colourful bougainvilleas and enhanced by the soothing notes of traditional music, the distant chirping of birds wafting through the crisp morning air, and the surreal sight of majestic peacocks.gold-more-icon
RIDE WAVES
At Taj Exotica Resort & Spa, Goa, adrenaline-seekers can consult our concierge for recommendations before diving into a thrilling itinerary of jet-skiing, parasailing, adventure, aqua zorbing and more.gold-more-icon
WATCH TOWER DINING
Taj Fisherman’s Cove Resort & Spa, Chennai offers a hidden, dreamy four-course feast in a charming thatched gazebo, with sweeping views of the vibrant Bay of Bengal. This fine-dining adventure is straight out of a dream—get ready for an unforgettable experience.gold-more-icon
Sundowners at Alsigarh
Taj Aravali Resort & Spa, Udaipur sets the scene at Alsigarh, one of Udaipur’s hidden lakes, where a magical table setting offers seclusion and spectacular views — the perfect spot to clink flutes of in-house wine with canapés under a rose-tinted sky.gold-more-icon
The Mangroves on Canvas
Sip on gin-based cocktails at Taj The Trees, Mumbai and unleash your creativity amidst serene mangrove views. Let the tranquil backdrop inspire your brushstrokes as you create a masterpiece that reflects the beauty of your surroundings, blending art and nature in a captivating experience.gold-more-icon
CShikara Dining
Indulge in the ultimate Shikara Dining experience, where the beauty of Kashmir unfolds around you in an exquisitely crafted meal for two. Set upon an authentic shikhara at The Deck, this unforgettable experience immerses you in panoramic views of the legendary Dal Lake and the majestic Zabarwan Mountains.gold-more-icon
BACKWATER RAFTING
Taj Bekal Resort & Spa, Kerala invites you to embark on a culinary journey with the Chef's Choice Menu—select two delectable appetizers paired with a fine bottle of wine. Complete the experience with an artisanal cheese platter, enhancing your backwater rafting adventure with exquisite flavours.gold-more-icon
FLOATING BREAKFAST
Elevate your morning at Taj Bekal Resort & Spa, Kerala with our exclusive floating breakfast experience in the comfort of your room. Delight in a Baker’s Basket with home preserves, jams, pancakes or waffles, a fresh fruit platter and refreshing seasonal juice for the perfect start to the day.gold-more-icon
Mini Bakers
At Rambagh Palace, Jaipur, our interactive, lively cookie-baking sessions are designed for your creative junior chefs to hone their skills under the guidance of experienced chefs.gold-more-icon
Croquet Quest
Challenge yourself to a spirited game of croquet at Rambagh Palace, Jaipur, where every mallet swing unveils twists on the lawn of chance, blending charm and thrill outdoors. Introduced to the palace by Her Highness Rajmata Gayatri Devi after discovering it during her early visits to England, this deceptively simple game is enthralling.gold-more-icon
Vintage Car Ride
Experience royalty and luxury with a cruise through Jaipur in a rare vintage automobile from the Rambagh Palace, Jaipur fleet. Let a knowledgeable chauffeur guide you past architectural marvels, majestic gardens, and splendid landmarks in timeless style.gold-more-icon
Contemporary Kaavad
Taj Amer, Jaipur revives Kaavad Banchana — a modern take on Rajasthan’s audio-visual storytelling tradition, where an expert raconteur captivates children with tales and educational games in immersive sessions.gold-more-icon
Muffin Marvels BOUGAINVILLEA GARDEN
Taj Bengal, Kolkata invites your little one’s creativity to blossom in a delightful 90-minute muffin-decorating session for 5 to 10-year-old children. Our experienced chef guides them, sharing tips, tricks, and plenty of chuckles between frosting swirls and sprinkles galore.gold-more-icon
Movie Nights BOUGAINVILLEA GARDEN
Taj Santacruz, Mumbai brings magical cinema nights for your kids — featuring handpicked blockbusters, cozy seating, and comfort snacks. This ‘red-carpet experience’ enchants little cinephiles while parents enjoy a peaceful intermission.gold-more-icon
THE GANGAUR: PRIVILEGED DINNER CRUISE
At Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur, indulge in a magical candlelit dining experience aboard the Gangaur, the 150-year-old, hand-oared personal barge of the Maharanas, featured in a classic James Bond film and countless royal festivals — an unforgettable offering.gold-more-icon
Kayaking Thrills
Taj Fort Aguada Resort & Spa, Goa invites you on a kayaking adventure along the tranquil Zuari River — guided by expert local partners and revealing secluded coves, unique flora and fauna, and other delightful discoveries curated exclusively for you.gold-more-icon
Artisan Picnic
Taj Mahal, New Delhi invites you to steal a few precious hours with your partner for an old-school romantic escape in the heart of a bustling metropolis. Savour gourmet refreshments from a curated hamper spread across our sun-drenched lawns and gardens during this picturesque picnic designed for uninterrupted ‘we-time’.gold-more-icon
Mixing It Up at Harbour Bar
Our Signature cocktail -From the Harbour Since 1933, has an intriguing origin story. During a cocktail making session with the Beverage Master, you can enjoy the charm and history of the speakeasy-style bar, that still holds License Number One.​gold-more-icon
